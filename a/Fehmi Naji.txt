Fehmi Naji El-Imam AM (Arabic: فهمي ناجي الإمام) (1928- 24 September 2016)  was the Grand Mufti of Australia from June 2007 to September 2011. Born in Lebanon, he arrived in Australia in 1951.  He was elected to succeed Taj El-Din Hilaly on 10 June 2007, but by January 2011 Hilaly declared that Naji was no longer active, and that de facto he was functioning as mufti.  Naji was succeeded on 18 September 2011 by Ibrahim Abu Mohamed.Naji questioned Osama bin Laden's responsibility for the September 11 attacks. He was the Head Imam of Preston Mosque in Melbourne and secretary of the Victorian Board of Imams.He died peacefully on 24 September 2016.
