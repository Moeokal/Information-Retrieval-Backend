The Farglory 95rich (Chinese: 遠雄九五) is a mixed-use skyscraper located in Xinzhuang District, New Taipei, Taiwan. The height of building is 184.4 m (605 ft), the floor area is 69,637.45 m2 (749,571.3 sq ft), and it comprises 42 floors above ground, as well as 5 basement levels. The building was completed in 2017. As of February 2021, it is the fourteenth tallest building in Taiwan and the third tallest in New Taipei City (after Far Eastern Mega Tower, Neo Sky Dome).
