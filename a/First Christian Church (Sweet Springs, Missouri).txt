First Christian Church is a historic Christian church located at 400 Bridge Street in Sweet Springs, Saline County, Missouri. It was built in 1882–1883, and is a one-story frame building measuring 45 feet by 60 feet.  It is sheathed in weatherboard and features stained glass lancet windows and a two-story, square, bell tower.: 2 It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.
