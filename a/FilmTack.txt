FilmTack is a multinational window films company headquartered in Singapore that develops and supports a wide range of products and services related to glass tinting and paint protection. 
The Company invests in related assets and businesses with a strong presence in the key global markets: Oceania, NE Asia (ex. China), ASEAN and North America. In addition to its home base in Singapore. FilmTack, through its subsidiaries, associates and joint ventures, is mainly engaged in product development of window films and paint protection films; trading of glare-reduction, HEV-Light protection and Infrared-filtering chemicals, antimicrobial window films, and provision of private-label branding services.
The group subsidiary, FilmTack Pte Ltd is a recipient of the E50.startup Award in 2006
 organized by Accenture, The Business Times and Economic Development Board of Singapore.
