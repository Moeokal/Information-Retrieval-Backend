Feng Keng (Chinese: 馮鏗; 1907–1931) was a poet and author who, following her execution became known as one of the Five Martyrs of the League of Left-Wing Writers. Feng was born in Guangdong province, China. Her mother was a teacher.When the magazine China Forum reported on the executions, it also published poems and stories written by four of those killed, Feng's work was featured.
