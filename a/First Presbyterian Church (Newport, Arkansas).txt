The First Presbyterian Church is a historic church building at 4th and Main Streets in Newport, Arkansas  It was designed by architects Sanders & Ginocchio in Classical Revival style and built in 1910.  It is a single-story brick structure, with a shallow hip roof over its main hall.  The entrance is set in a slightly projecting gable-ended section, flanked by Tuscan columns and square pilasters.The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
