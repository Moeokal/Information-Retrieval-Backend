Faktor A (in Russian Фактор А) is the second Russian version of The X Factor television talent show franchise. Having broadcast on Rossiya 1from 2011 to 2013, the show is named Faktor A (rather than Faktor X), with the letter "A" chosen in honor of Alla Pugacheva, head of the jury. Faktor A debuted in 2011 as a new season 1.
