Farndish is a rural hamlet in northwest Bedfordshire, located about 500 metres (yards) east of the county border with Northamptonshire. The village is near the Northamptonshire villages of Irchester and Wollaston and the Bedfordshire village of Wymington. The name Farndish means fern-clad pasture. It is in the civil parish of Podington.
