Discover the Networks (originally Discover the Network) (DtN)  is a website run by the David Horowitz Freedom Center that focuses on the individuals, groups, and history of groups perceived to be politically left wing. DtN was launched in 2004 and has a staff of about a dozen contributors. Its current Editor-in-Chief is David Horowitz; John Perazzo is the project's managing editor, and Richard Poe is its investigative editor. Discover the Networks is associated with FrontPage Magazine.
