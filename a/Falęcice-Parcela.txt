Falęcice-Parcela [falɛnˈt͡ɕit͡sɛ parˈt͡sɛla] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Promna, within Białobrzegi County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-west of Promna, 9 km (6 mi) north-west of Białobrzegi, and 56 km (35 mi) south of Warsaw.
